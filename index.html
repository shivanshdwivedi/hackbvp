About Hack@BVP 4.0

Hackathon: Hack@BVP 4.0 is a 12-hour hackathon. Its mission is to provide a thrilling experience to students, developers, and anyone interesting in coding. Teams of developers, designers, and marketers will come together to build functioning apps, websites, programs or anything that tackle COVID-19 issues in just 12 hours.


The outbreak of a disease like COVID-19 can create huge challenges for the government, workers in healthcare, businesses, educational institutions etc. In such a situation, a little effort from every individual can go a long way. The motivation behind the themes for this hackathon is the current COVID-19 situation; to prepare ourselves to deal with the pandemic and to march forward, creating new ideas and working on them.

Hack, Invent and Create.

A 12 hour Hackathon aimed at exploring your zeal to innovate and challenge your software development skills! Shout out to all computer nerds and gizmo geeks.

Commerce

Today, business owners are threatened by the overwhelming challenges posed by the pandemic. The business sector has seen a tremendous downfall with buyers locked up at their places with the uncertainty created by the pandemic looming over them. Markets have shut down, completely diverting the focus of buyers towards e-commerce. Minimal expenditure for just the essentials has become the new normal. Time to enjoy luxuries are almost over. So, with this theme, we aim to explore innovation in the field of e-commerce and business.

Education

COVID-19 has greatly affected the educational sector. Education has been hit particularly hard by the pandemic, with 1.53 billion learners having to drop out of school and 184 country-wide school closures. Therefore, continuing education through alternative learning pathways is one of the biggest concerns right now, to limit the blow to education is as much as possible. You can develop software/hardware that can be accessed and used by the people even in remote areas to ensure that nobody has to say no to something as basic as education. Educators too, face trouble while teaching online, calling for a simpler solution. Let's "Make in India"!

HealthCare

With the exponentially increasing impact of the virus, people in the healthcare industry are required to be very cautious and supportive towards patients, having to work overtime and staying away from their own families. Despite their efforts, cases are rising non-stop, making it more and more difficult to arrange resources for everyone, evidently putting the industry in dire need of all the assistance it can get.

Lifestyle

COVID-19 has affected all spheres of life and livelihood, affecting our lifestyle adversely. Not only has the danger of the disease made us more cautious, but the containment measures taken in the wake of the pandemic have substantially changed our way of living. Thus, the pandemic has made us shape our lives differently than before. The constant need to wash hands, maintain social distancing, choose home-made food are now inseparable features of our lifestyle. Developing apps or devices that can detect crowded areas or places for you to avoid to ensure social distancing in public places can improve the quality of life. (This is just an example, the possibilities are endless!) So, with this theme, we aim to explore lifestyle changes in these times of restricted mobility due to COVID-19 in India.

Security

The world struggling with the impact of COVID-19 appears as an opportunity for cybercriminals. As countries go into lockdown mode, forcing citizens work and study at home, cyber-security becomes a major issue. As a consequence of this, there has been a rapid increase in cyber attacks in multiple forms, ranging from ransomware attacks, remote access network, spear-phishing, secretly deploy malware etc. The pandemic has created an enormous challenge for businesses worldwide: to continue operating despite massive shutdowns of offices and other facilities. The technologies on which they have long depended (their data centres, cloud systems, departmental servers and digital devices) have become even more vital. The government too, has become more dependent on digital mediums to reassure the public and maintain order. It is expected to make rules, share critical information and contain the spread of misinformation. They are using these digital channels to urge employers to pay salaries and the public to avoid hoarding essential resources along with avoiding spreading rumours. This is only a snippet of why it has become imperative to develop efficient security systems to safeguard confidential data. We invite innovators to create smart solutions for the same.

FAQ

Who can participate?

Anyone who loves to learn and build cool stuff can attend. Whether you have a background in data science, programming, design or business – we want to invite a diverse community to have fun and solve interesting problems!

What is the duration of the hackathon?

It is a 12 Hour long Online Hackathon.

What is the goal?

The primary goal is to have fun, connect with new people and build something cool. The secondary goal is to solve an exciting and challenging problem that could impact thousands of lives every day.

I don’t have a team. Can I still join?

We are currently only looking for complete teams. We encourage you to reach out to your friends and colleagues at your university or work.

What will it cost me to participate?

Participation is free.

On what platform will the hackathon take place?

Microsoft Teams

What challenges lie in store for the teams?

The challenges are based on real-life problems that MAN and other players in the transport industry face on a day-to-day basis. We provide rich real-world data and descriptions of unsolved problems which you are free to choose from.
